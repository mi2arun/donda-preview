Module 01

Overview & Scope

The diamond-trading business cycle Donda ERP supports, the actors involved, the end-to-end lifecycle, and what changed in version 2.1.

Business Overview

Donda ERP serves diamond manufacturers who buy rough diamonds at international expos/tenders, import them, and either process them in-house or transfer/sell them to sister companies or third parties. The "Donda group" is a set of sister companies under one owner in different countries (e.g. Rainbow Gems HK Ltd, Donda Exports).

Management attends expos (Belgium, Botswana, South Africa, Canada…) where each event showcases ~600 lots over several weeks. The team inspects and bids on hand-picked lots daily; winners pay on the spot, and the diamonds are handed to a secure courier for shipment to the buying company.

Scope

In scope (current phase)

  • Bidding data backend & API — the ERP stores all expo data and serves the bidding mobile app API
  • Won Lots — derived from bid wins (manual entry also supported)
  • Inbound logistics — shipments from expo to the buying company
  • Purchase invoice with expense allocation & auto-created rough lots
  • Rough Lot Master with group-level auto numbering and sub-lots
  • Sales invoice — third-party sale or sister-company transfer, with sub-lot splitting
  • Inter-company transfer, outbound logistics, and auto accounting

Out of scope (this phase)

  • The bidding mobile app UI — built by a separate team; ERP provides data/API only
  • Manufacturing / polishing module
  • Consignment & Memo rough trading FUTURE
  • India local sales GST / HSN computation FUTURE
  • Live courier API integration (manual tracking entry for now)
