3. Multi-Origin Shipment Example

A single shipment (AWB 340567) consolidating parcels from 3 different countries and 3 supplier invoices — based on the real documents provided

Shipment: HAWB 340567
Courier: Ferrari Belgium BVBA | Route: Brussels → ZRH → HKG | KPC: EU 00979933

3 Sealed Parcels (Seal: .M2461456)

Parcel 1 — South Africa

Invoice #20260578

Cullinan Mine (Petra Diamonds)

2 lots: CDM-2604-024, CDM-2604-053

503.31 CT | $82,139.17

Parcel 2 — Lesotho

Invoice #20260645

KAO Mine (Storm Mountain)

3 lots: KAO-2602-070, 085, 086

257.34 CT | $122,697.07

Parcel 3 — Canada

Invoice #20260340

Gahcho Kué (Mountain Province)

1 lot: MPV-2602-052

2.75 CT | $9,292.64

Total: 763.40 CT | $214,128.88 USD
What happens in ERP on receipt:
  1. Shipment status → Delivered
  2. 3 Purchase Invoices created (one per supplier invoice)
  3. 6 Rough Lots auto-created (2 + 3 + 1 parcels)
  4. Each lot's inventory value = purchase price + proportional expenses
  5. All 6 lots in Rough Stock, ready for sale/transfer

5. Document Flow Comparison

Inbound (Purchase) vs Outbound (Sales) — parallel document flows

INBOUND — Purchase Flow

1
Supplier Invoice Received e.g., Invoice #20260645 from Bonas Couzyn
2
House Airway Bill HAWB 340567 — routing, seal nos, declared value
3
Kimberley Process Certificate EU 00979933 — conflict-free compliance
4
Purchase Invoice (ERP) Created in system with all details + expenses
5
Rough Lot Master (Auto) Inventory record per parcel, valued at cost + expenses
6
AP Journal Entry (Auto) Dr. Purchase + Expenses | Cr. Supplier Payable

OUTBOUND — Sales Flow

1
Select Rough Lots to Sell Pick from available stock (In Stock status)
2
Create Sales Invoice Inter-company or third-party, set selling price
3
Export KPC Obtained New KPC for outbound compliance
4
Outbound Shipment Created Courier assigned, AWB generated
5
Inventory Updated Rough lot status → Sold, removed from stock
6
AR Journal Entry (Auto) Dr. Receivable | Cr. Sales + COGS

7. Daily Expo Cycle

How the bidding and handover cycle repeats each day during a multi-week expo

🔍
MORNING

Team inspects lots, selects targets

✍️
SUBMIT BIDS

Place bids on selected lots

📢
WINNERS

Expo announces winners

💳
PAY

Payment collected on the spot

📦
HANDOVER

Diamonds → courier with buyer address

↻ This cycle repeats daily for the duration of the expo (typically 2-4 weeks)
